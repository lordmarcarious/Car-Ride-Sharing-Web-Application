Introduction:
This web application is developed as a car ride-sharing web application where there are two entities: Customer and Driver. 
* Customers can request a ride from location A to B, where they will be shown the fare automatically for the ride they requested. They can also see the history of the rides that they took in the past. At the end of the ride, they can rate the drivers and also leave behind a review.
*Drivers can see the rides available, in which they will be shown the details of the customer, fare, distance, and the pickup and drop-off location, while also being able to chat with the customer. They can see the total amount of money that they earned and also file a complaint.

How to run:
To run the Web Application, you need to have XAMPP (Watch YouTube Tutorial) installed on your computer. After you have installed XAMPP, paste "http://localhost/Car-Ride-Sharing-Web-Application/index.php" in your browser, and voila, you have successfully found yourself at the login page
